ABOUT US

“Painting is the silence of the thought and the music of the sight.”
The Fine Arts Club is a committed team of artists who strive to promote the various forms of fine art amongst the campus junta. At the Fine Arts Club, we believe that art lets us free ourselves and discover ourselves within. We work towards building a forum that fosters growth among the budding artists in the campus. The club persistently tries to lend out a hand to make a positive impact amongst the community, bringing out cognizance over the present-day social issues through our expertise in art.
ArtFest is the annual flagship event organised by the Fine Arts Club. The motive of the event is to bring art out of the club room and on the streets to make it engaging for all. Fine Arts Club, in coordination with the Design Club, organises several workshops which are open for anyone living in the campus and includes various levels of pencil sketching workshops, clay modelling workshops, calligraphy and knife painting workshops. Various summer projects/activities on wall-painting, calligraphy, typography, and artforms involving other mediums (clay, charcoal, soft-pastels etc) are arranged by the club to explore the field of art. Apart from serving the campus residents, the club also conducts workshops for art-enthusiasts outside the campus.
Wanna join us? Your enthusiasm and passion is all that we stand in need of. For further details regarding the happenings of the club, check out our social media handles.

RECENTS EVENTS

  1. Freshers' Night
    This occasion marks the first contact point between freshers and the club. Various art performances are given by freshers' batch on this day which provides a platform to exhibit their talents.
  2. Workshops
    Fine Arts club is dedicated to offering a free and helpful service to the IIT Kanpur campus students and faculty, to those interested in art, providing a showcase for amateur artists. Apart from free service the club is also a big platform for those who want to learn and develop skills and ideas.
  1. Inter IIT/College Events
    The Fine Arts Club annually takes part in multiple inter college events (eg. Inter-IIT etc.) and is able to achieve many laurels.
  2. Art Fest
    ArtFest is the annual flagship event organized by Fine Arts Club. The purpose of the event is to bring art out of the club room and on to the streets to make it engaging for all. As a part of the 'festivities', several installations are made on the theme and placed at popular spots across the campus with the idea of making art interactive.
